The Ronin Society

The Ronin Society helps growth-minded enterpreneurs around the world increase their chance of achieving lasting success, reduce their odds of failure, and achieve their vision and full potential.

We lead the small market globally by promoting the highest standards of ethics, education, and professional excellence for the ultimate benefit of society while working on the invnestment side to create more fertile conditions for investment in small to medium sized business by raising the bar for transparency and efficiency.

As a Strategist I work on a long-term basis to advise founders on how to grow sustainably using financial analysis as a backdrop.

Konara Homes

Konara Homes is a cohousing company in Austin, TX. We create great places to live. Our homes are furnished and each room is leased out individually across flexible lease terms. We aim to provide excellent service and promote a community where people are supported, nurtured and encouraged.

Konara comes from the latin roots for "shared hearth" and the long term vision for Konara is to be an example for and component of the Equity Economy.
